Lawrence's Plays

  • Many plays; little appreciated in his lifetime
  • A Collier's Friday Night (1909)
    • Naturalistic play
    • Ironic allusion to Burns's sentimental “The Cotter'sSaturday Night”
    • Plot similar to Sons and Lovers
  • The Daughter-in-Law (1912)
    • Searing portrayal of a marriage in crisis
  • The Widowing of Mrs Holroyd (1914)
    • A play based on 19th century domestic life
    • Story of a wife and mother trying desperately to make a safe home amidst the coarseness of a coal mining village in England
  • Touch and Go
  • David
    • Unlike the other plays
    • Old Testament story
    • David brings down two giants, Goliath and Saul
